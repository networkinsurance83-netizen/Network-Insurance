This website provides general educational information only. It does not provide legal, tax, medical, investment, or financial advice.

Insurance products, availability, eligibility, premiums, benefits, exclusions, limitations, provider networks, prescription coverage, formularies, underwriting, and plan rules vary by state, carrier, plan, household, age, health history, and eligibility. Brad Smith may not represent every carrier or product available.

Submitting a form, requesting a quote, requesting a coverage review, or scheduling a consultation does not create coverage, bind coverage, or guarantee approval, savings, eligibility, pricing, provider access, or benefits. Coverage is not effective until approved and confirmed by the insurance carrier.

Life insurance products may require underwriting and may include exclusions, contestability periods, surrender charges, policy fees, cost-of-insurance charges, loan interest, lapse risk, and tax considerations. Indexed universal life and cash-value illustrations are not guarantees. Actual performance, credited interest, policy values, and death benefits can differ from illustrations and depend on policy funding, charges, index caps, participation rates, floors, loans, withdrawals, and carrier rules.

Supplemental products such as dental, vision, accident, critical illness, hospital indemnity, and disability income protection are limited-benefit policies. They are not a substitute for major medical health insurance unless the official policy documents say otherwise.

Visitors should review official policy documents, plan summaries, evidence of coverage, provider directories, formularies, illustrations, benefit schedules, exclusions, limitations, and carrier materials before applying or relying on coverage details.

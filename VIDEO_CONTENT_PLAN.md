# Network Insurance Video Content Plan

Purpose: short educational videos for the website, Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, YouTube Shorts, and future HeyGen avatar content.

## Style

- Length: 30-60 seconds each.
- Tone: clear, friendly, plain English.
- CTA: book a coverage review with an advisor.
- Compliance: no promises of savings, approval, eligibility, provider access, or health outcomes.

## Video 1 - What Is a Deductible?

Hook: `Ever see the word deductible and wonder what it really means?`

Script:
`A deductible is the amount you may need to pay for certain covered services before your plan starts sharing some costs. Not every service works the same way, and preventive care may be handled differently depending on the plan. The key is to compare the deductible with the premium, doctor network, prescriptions, and out-of-pocket maximum. If you want help reviewing those pieces, book a coverage review with an advisor at Network Insurance. Options and costs vary by plan and location.`

## Video 2 - Out-of-Pocket Maximum

Hook: `This number can matter a lot when medical bills get expensive.`

Script:
`The out-of-pocket maximum is the yearly limit on certain covered in-network costs you may pay before the plan pays more of eligible covered services. It usually does not include premiums or non-covered care. When comparing plans, this number can help you understand possible risk if you have a bigger medical year. Book a coverage review with an advisor if you want help comparing your options. Coverage and eligibility vary.`

## Video 3 - HMO vs PPO vs EPO

Hook: `HMO, PPO, and EPO are really about networks and flexibility.`

Script:
`An HMO often uses a tighter provider network and may require referrals. A PPO may offer more flexibility, sometimes with different costs for out-of-network care. An EPO often focuses on in-network care except emergencies. The best fit depends on your doctors, prescriptions, budget, and how much flexibility you want. An advisor can help review your options. Plan rules vary by carrier and location.`

## Video 4 - Why Primary Care Matters

Hook: `Your primary doctor can be one of the most important parts of your health routine.`

Script:
`Regular checkups may help you stay on top of preventive care, screenings, and important health questions. A health plan can also affect which doctors are in network and what services may be covered. Before choosing coverage, review doctor access, preventive care, prescription needs, and total costs. Book a coverage review with an advisor at Network Insurance. This is general education, not medical advice.`

## Video 5 - Preventing Medical Debt Surprises

Hook: `Medical bills can affect savings fast.`

Script:
`Health coverage may help manage certain medical costs, but every plan has different premiums, deductibles, copays, networks, and out-of-pocket limits. That is why it is important to compare the full picture, not just the monthly price. If you want a plain-English review, book a time with an advisor. Options, eligibility, and costs vary by plan and location.`

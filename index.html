Quick education

Coverage terms people ask about most

Deductible

The amount you usually pay before a plan starts sharing certain covered costs. Details vary by plan.

Out-of-pocket maximum

A yearly limit on certain covered costs. Premiums and non-covered services usually do not count.

HMO

A plan type that often uses a specific provider network and may require referrals for specialists.

PPO

A plan type that may offer more provider flexibility, often with different in-network and out-of-network costs.

EPO

A plan type that often focuses on in-network care, except emergencies. Network details matter.

Preventive care

Routine checkups and screenings may help identify health concerns earlier, depending on the service and plan.
